# Sahyadri College Building Information

## Department Locations
- CSE (Computer Science and Engineering) department is located on the ground floor
- AIML (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) department is located on the 1st floor
- ISE (Information Science and Engineering) department is located on the 2nd floor
- Robotics and Mechanical department is located on the 4th floor

## Lab Locations
- CAD lab is located on the 5th floor
- EC Lab (Electronics and Communication Lab) is located on the 5th floor

## Facilities
- Seminar halls are available on each floor, but only up to the 2nd floor
- Washrooms are available on each floor
- The lift is exclusively for faculty use only, students are not allowed to use it

## Administrative Offices
- The principal's office is currently under renovation
- A temporary principal's office has been set up adjacent to the old principal's office
- The temporary office is located near the GD (Group Discussion) room
